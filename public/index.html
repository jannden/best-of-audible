Looking for great books on Audible?

The problem: When you filter books by Average Customer Review on Audible, you are supplied with results that have high averages from few reviews. What use is having an average rating of 5 stars if only 10 people voted? The average ratings become meaningful after at least hundreds of people have voted.

The solution: This tool will filters books from a specific category and with a minimal average rating from a minimal number of reviewers.
